Language-Based Artificial Intelligence Lab
We work at the intersection of programming languages and artificial intelligence (AI), i.e., take a language-based approach to building intelligent systems. We focus on applications to science and mathematics. Our work draws on techniques from programming languages, AI, and machine learning.
Research
  • Probabilistic Programming for Modeling Atomistic Systems. Projects involve building probabilistic programming languages, automatic differentiation tools, and machine learning models for modeling atomistic systems.
  • Intelligent Systems for Mathematics. Projects involve constructing AI systems for reasoning about mathematics expressed in formal languages.
