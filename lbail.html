Language-Based Artificial Intelligence Lab
We study the intersection of programming languages and artificial intelligence (AI) techniques. We apply this technology to science problems, i.e., AI for science. We also experiment with emerging hardware (e.g., quantum computing). Please contact danehuang [at] sfsu [dot] edu if interested in doing research in the areas listed below.
Research Areas
  • AI for Science. Projects involve applying AI (e.g., probabilistic programming) to science applications (e.g., chemistry).
  • Probabilistic Programming. Projects involve building novel probabilistic programming systems (e.g., for Bayesian Deep Learning).
  • Neuro-Symbolic AI. Projects involve constructing deep learning systems for reasoning about mathematics expressed in formal languages.
  • Quantum Computing. Projects involve working on compilers to take advantage of emerging quantum hardware.
