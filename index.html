About
I am an assistant professor of computer science at San Francisco State University. I work at the intersection of programming languages and machine learning. In the past, I have been fortunate to work with Professor Jean-Baptiste Tristan (at Boston College), Professor Dawn Song (at UC Berkeley), and Professor Greg Morrisett for my PhD (at Harvard).
